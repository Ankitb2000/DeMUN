Introduction

Delhi Public School, Bangalore East is proud to present to you it's 5th edition of it's Model United Nations confernce - DeMUN. DeMUN started out has a humble internal conference back in 2014. This year marks the second time when the MUN will be attended by delegates from schools other than DPS East. DeMUN 2018 is back this year bigger and better than ever before on the 2nd and 3rd of August. Hope to see you all there!
